THE FUN THEY HAD

Question [CLICK ON ANY CHOICE TO KNOW THE RIGHT ANSWER]
From which point of view is “THE FUN THEY HAD” told?
A
First Person (the narrator is a character in the story)
B
Second Person
C
Third person (the narrator is not a character in the story)
D
None of the above
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -In third person point of view the narrator is not part of the story and the characters never acknowledge the narrator’s presence.

Detailed explanation-2: -There are two types of third-person point of view: omniscient, in which the narrator knows all of the thoughts and feelings of all of the characters in the story, or limited, in which the narrator relates only their own thoughts, feelings, and knowledge about various situations and the other characters.

Detailed explanation-3: -Third-person point of view means that the narrator is not in the story. The third-person narrator is not a character. They are like the reader; they are outside of the story and watching it unfold.

Detailed explanation-4: -In third-person point of view, the author is narrating a story about the characters, referring to them by name, or using the third-person pronouns “he, ” “she, ” and “they.” The other points of view in writing are first person and second person.

Detailed explanation-5: -Interactive Literature Selections The Fun They Had. Third-Person Point of View. In a story told from the third-person point of view, the narrator does not take part in the action and tells the story using words such as he and she instead of I and we.
